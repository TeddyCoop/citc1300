The Music of The Nightmare Before Christmas

The unforgettable music and soundtrack of The Nightmare Before Christmas were composed by the legendary Danny Elfman. The film's songs and score are integral to its atmosphere and storytelling, making it a timeless classic for both Halloween and Christmas.

About Danny Elfman

Danny Elfman is a renowned composer and songwriter, famous for his collaborations with director Tim Burton. He composed the score for The Nightmare Before Christmas and provided the singing voice for Jack Skellington. His unique musical style perfectly captures the eerie and whimsical tone of the film.
