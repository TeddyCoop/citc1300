About Loathe

The Beginning

Loathe is a British heavy metal band that started in Liverpool in 2014. They quickly gained attention with their mix of metalcore, post-metal, and industrial sounds. Their music combines heavy riffs with atmospheric elements, creating a unique sound. This blend has helped them stand out in the metal scene. Over the years, Loathe has built a strong fanbase and become known for pushing the limits of heavy music. From the beginning, they've been dedicated to creating something different.

The Rise

Since forming, Loathe has released several albums that have been praised by critics. Each album shows how the band continues to experiment with new sounds. Fans love how they mix heavy music with melody, making their songs both intense and emotional. Their live shows are known for their powerful energy and deep emotions. Loathe's focus on innovation has made them one of the most exciting bands in metal today. Their music and performances have earned them a strong reputation in the metal community.
