Technical Specifications

The Corvette C5 is renowned for its exceptional engineering. Below are key specifications that highlight its performance and features.

Engine

5.7L LS1 V8, producing 350 hp and 360 lb-ft of torque.

Transmission

Available with a 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission.

Dimensions

Length: 176.5 in, Width: 74.0 in, Height: 48.5 in.

Weight

Approximately 3,200 lbs, providing excellent power-to-weight ratio.

Performance

0-60 mph in around 5 seconds, top speed of 175 mph.

Fuel Economy

22 mpg city / 30 mpg highway, impressive for a sports car.
